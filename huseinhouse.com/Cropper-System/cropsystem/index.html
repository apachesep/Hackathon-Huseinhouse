Positive characteristics

-bees are attracted to flowers with blue and white

-birds are attracted to flowers with violet, blue, green and RED (anything with red hue, orange, purple, yellow etc.)

-VIOLET, it has red hue and white

-Single flowers — those with one ring of petals — provide more nectar and pollen

-Double flowers have extra petals have replaced pollen-laden anthers

-Double flowers also make it more difficult for bees to reach the inner flower parts

-Lines and coloration on petals help pollinators quickly find flowers
